## Beyond Tokens · 5 essays · complete

![Beyond Tokens](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MaverickHQ/beyond-tokens/main/docs/beyond_tokens_readme.png)

Why world models matter — and why token-based AI fails when decisions
must persist over time.

The core argument: modern AI failures are almost never the result of bad
models. They are almost always architectural mismatches. The alternative:
systems built around explicit world models with persistent state, defined
dynamics, and verifiable transitions.
